    Application Re-Engineering & Mordenization

    Maintaining and upgrading legacy systems is one of the most challenging tasks faced today, including limitations of adding new capabilities and features for the new-age work styles and business models, multiple technology platforms, high maintenance costs, unsupported systems , diminishing employee expertise, and lack of web capabilities.

    Constraints of budget, time, and resource and risk management compel business to modernize legacy system by implementing one of the strategies below:

    • Simply update and optimize performance of current system.
    • Keep the business logic of application on legacy application while providing user a more friendly interface to the data(GUI screens or Web Browser)
    • Integrate legacy application including business logic with new technologies while data resides on legacy system.
    • Migrate part of legacy application to different platform with little or no change to core business logic.

    Reveille’s end-to-end transformation of legacy applications to newer technologies and architectures help companies to mitigate cost and risks.

    Reveille Re-Engineering services include:

    • Legacy Database & Platform Migration
    • Application Re-engineering/li>
    • .Net Architecture Design
    • Reverse Engineering to generate system specification
    • Data Migration & Verification
    • Migration to multiple databases or database upgrade/migration to web platforms

    Reveille provide cost effective migration to new technologies while extending the life cycle of their functionally-rich existing legacy software.

    Mergers and Acquisitions

    The mergers and acquisitions (M&A) transaction process is fraught with pitfalls and roadblocks that should be carefully navigated with skill and care to achieve the goal of one new improved organization from two separate, distinctly different companies. Of all these pitfalls, the one that frequently poses a challenge is how to plan for and execute the post-merger IT systems integration.

    We work with clients to assess existing IT environments and capabilities, and then identify the opportunities and threats of changes in business structure through our due diligence process. We work together to develop a tailored strategic IT roadmap and migration plan, and to guide the IT decisions and actions required to realize the deal’s maximum value.

    Reveille brings to you not only our extensive knowledge base and hands-on efforts in post merger integration, M&A due diligence and post-deal restructuring, but also its combined skills and leadership from our staff.
